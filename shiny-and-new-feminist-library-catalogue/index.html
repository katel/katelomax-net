    Shiny and new Feminist Library catalogue

    June 1, 2018

    I recently worked with the Feminist Library to upgrade and migrate their Koha-based Library Management System. 

    We made quite a leap from version Koha 3.12 to 17.11 and the new, shiny and responsive (!) catalogue is now available at: 

    https://catalogue.feministlibrary.co.uk

    I haven't worked with Koha LMS much recently so it's good to be back and see all the great work that's happened on one of the best and friendliest open source projects in libraryland. 

    And, digging through my notes, it looks like the first time I installed Koha for the Feminist Library was way back in  2008! 

    Looking forward to seeing their collection continue to expand and evolve.
