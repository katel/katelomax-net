    Announcing Making Library Makers

    January 30, 2017

    From the launch announcement:

    It's challenging thing to deliver training for makers and making online - we've approached this by focusing on providing a friendly and supportive online learning environment in which learners can tackle real projects that are relevant to the type of library they work in. And they have access to trainers to get help and ask questions at every step of the way.

    If you want to find out more about Making Library Makers, check it out at www.makinglibrarymakers.com.

    And you can read the whole blog post over on the Artefacto website.
