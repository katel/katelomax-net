    Upcoming digital maker workshops & talks

    May 30, 2016

    There are lots of great maker events happening over the summer.

    I'm super excited to be part of the Institute of Imagination Mini Maker Faire at OxoTowerWharf on the 11 and 12th of June. I'll be there to help out with the Makercart, a mobile pop-up makerspace for schools and libraries with a 3d printer, digital cutter, electronics, micro-controllers and robotics.

    Artefacto will also be hosting a Digital Making Workshop at Redbridge Library for London Technology Week, find out more on the Redbridge Libraries website.

    And in July you can catch us at the CILIP conference where Carlos (of the awesome Makercart project) and I will be talking about the the Makercart, LibraryMakers and how to get a Makerspace up and running in your library.
